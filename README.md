# Projects
Financial Analysis Graduation Project
Vertical Analysis Income Statement & Balance Sheet CIRA Education & Taaleem Management Services

These 2 Power BI Dashboards contain Vertical Analysis Income Statement and Balance Sheet for both companies that are in private education sector CIRA Education and Taaleem Management Services from 2020 to 2024. The Income Statement Vertical Analysis shows the 5-year effect by expressing each item in the income statement as % of net sales revenue. As for the Balance Sheet Vertical Analysis, it shows the 5-year effect by expressing each item in balance sheet as a % of total assets.

I've used Power Query for data cleaning and preparation then used Power BI to create visualizations that express my 5-year Vertical Analysis Income Statement and Balance Sheet for CIRA Education and Taaleem Management Services where I've built dashboards using DAX new measures.

The purpose of the Vertical Analysis Dashboards is that they show us the relation between 2 companies that are in the same industry seeing how both companies are performing and expressing them using a base item.

These VA Income Statement Dashboards show us important highlights in:

1- The change of Gross Profit, Operating Income and Net Income (2021-2024) as % of net sales revenue.                                                                           
2- Revenue Composition (2020-2024)                                                                                                                                  
3- Margin Lines (2020-2024)                                                                                                                                                
4- Cost Efficiency (COGS vs Operating Expenses as % of net sales)                                                                                                           
5- Net Inome before taxes vs Net Income after taxes (2020-2024)                                                                                                      
6- Interest Expense vs Other Income (2020-2024)

For VA Balance Sheet Dashboards:

1- The change of Total Current Assets, Total Long-term Assets, Total Liabilities and Total Equity (2021-2024) as a % of total assets.                                       
2- Current Assets vs Current Liabilities as a % of total assets (2020-2024)                                                                                                 
3- Debt vs Equity Grwoth (2020-2024)                                                                                                                                 
4- Asset Structure (2020-2024)                                                                                                                                             
5- Liabilities vs Equity Structure (2020-2024)
